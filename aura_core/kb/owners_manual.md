# Vehicle Owner's Manual — Aura Driver Co-Pilot (excerpts)

## What Aura is
Aura is an edge-AI driver co-pilot. It recognizes who is driving, learns each driver's
personal fatigue limits, explains its decisions in plain language, and — as a last resort —
can safely take over and pull the vehicle over. It runs entirely inside the car.

## Getting started
When you sit down, the cabin camera identifies you and loads your profile: your playlist,
your preferred alert style, and your personal safety baseline. If you are not recognized, Aura
uses the conservative "Guest" profile until you enroll.

## Dashboard warning indicators
- Steady green shield: monitoring, all nominal.
- Blue chime icon: a gentle fatigue alert — take a breath, refocus.
- Amber steering icon: active assist engaged (lane-keep, reduced speed).
- Orange triangle: prepare for handover — pull over soon or let Aura assist.
- Red car icon "AURA ASSIST ENGAGED": Aura is taking over to pull over safely. Do not fight
  the wheel; the car is guiding itself to the roadside.

## What to do during a takeover
If AURA ASSIST ENGAGED appears, the vehicle is bringing itself to a safe stop because it
judged you unfit to continue. Keep your hands ready but let the car complete the maneuver. Once
stopped, rest. To resume, confirm you are alert (reopen your eyes and respond, or say
"I'm awake"); Aura will hand control back one step at a time.

## Alert modalities
You can set how Aura warns you: audio (spoken/chime), visual (dashboard nudge), or haptic
(seat/steering vibration). Choose the one you respond to fastest. Aura remembers it per driver.

## Adjusting your profile
Your personal drowsiness threshold adapts automatically as Aura learns your calm-driving
baseline. You can view it under Settings. The threshold is bounded for safety and cannot be
set to a value that would stop Aura from ever warning you.

## Data and privacy
Aura stores your profile and learned baseline on the vehicle only. Camera frames are processed
in real time and are not recorded or transmitted. No personal data leaves the car.

## Maintenance and limitations
Keep the cabin camera lens clean and unobstructed. In very low light, an infrared cabin camera
improves detection (available on equipped trims). Aura assists the driver and does not replace
attentive driving; the driver remains responsible except during an active safe-takeover.
